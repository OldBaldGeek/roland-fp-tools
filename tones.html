Roland FP-60/FP-90/FP-60X/FP-90X Tones

This page shows the Tones available on the Roland FP-60, FP-90, FP-60X, and FP-90X digital pianos.

  • "Name" is the Tone name as shown on the piano
  • "Location" is the location of the Tone: button, then index
  • "Category" is my attempt to group Tones as piano, bass, reed, etc.
  • "Modulation Amount" is the default amount of modulation for the Tone. An empty cell means there is no modulation effect for the Tone
  • "Modulation Effect" tells what the modulation does
  • "Rotary Effect" tells whether the Tone supports a rotary speaker effect
  • "Notes" contain general comments on the Tone
  • "MIDI..." shows the MIDI Bank and Program number for the Tone
